# Civilian Mission Profiles

## 1. The "Wide-Arc Search Pattern" (SAR)
Traditional SAR drones waste time making sharp, energy-bleeding banking turns to scan a grid. The AV-Apex flies straight at 850 km/h, scans a 50km corridor, and executes the 180° wide-arc inversion. It returns on the exact parallel track without losing speed, covering 3x the search area of a traditional propeller UAV.

## 2. Wildfire Border Patrol
Flying at maximum speed, the AV-Apex can circumnavigate a massive wildfire complex in minutes. Using the lateral 15–35° thrust vectoring, it can make micro-adjustments to its flight path to follow the shifting fire line without banking, keeping its thermal sensors perfectly level at all times.

## 3. 6,000 km Round-Tip Emergency Response
A wildfire or maritime disaster breaks out 3,000 km away. A Config C (6-engine) AV-Apex launches, cruises at Mach 0.7, arrives on station in under 4 hours, drops its external payload (supplies or life rafts), immediately executes the 180° inversion, and returns home without refueling. No propeller drone can achieve this transit time.
